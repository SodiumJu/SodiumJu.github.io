About Me

I am currently a Research Assistant at the Reinforcement Learning in Gaming Lab, Academia Sinica, Taiwan. My recent work focuses on MuZero and AlphaZero, with applications to both board games and Atari environments.

My current research interests include Mixture of Experts (MoE) models for gaming, imperfect-information games, and the application of large language models (LLMs) in games. Beyond gaming, I am also interested in broader research topics in reinforcement learning and the use of AI in biomedical domains.

I am currently seeking a PhD position. If you are interested, please feel free to contact me at yj5ju0bj@gmail.com.
